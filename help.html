Help & Support

Find answers to common questions about Modern Schooling

Getting Started

Modern Schooling helps you keep track of your learning, blog posts, activities, and calendar.

  • Use the Daily Blog to write about what you did each day.
  • Check the Calendar for upcoming assignments and events.
  • Visit Activities for extra practice and fun learning tasks.

Customising Your Experience

  • Change colours in Theme settings.
  • Make text bigger or smaller in Font Size.
  • Switch languages in Language settings.
  • Turn emails and reminders on or off in Notifications.
  • Update your details on the Account page.

Need More Help?

If you get stuck, ask your teacher or parent to look at this page with you. You can also write notes in your Daily Blog about what you find confusing so you remember to ask about it later.
