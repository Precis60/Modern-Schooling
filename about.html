About Lucas

Get to know the student behind Modern Schooling

Hi, I'm Lucas Anderson!

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm a Year 8 student who's passionate about learning, technology, and sharing my educational journey with others.

My Story

I started this blog because I believe that education today is fundamentally different from previous generations. We're learning with iPads, collaborating online, and discovering new ways to understand complex topics every day. I wanted to document this unique experience and maybe help other students along the way.

What I Love About School

  • Science: Especially chemistry and physics - there's something magical about understanding how the world works at a molecular level
  • Mathematics: I used to think it was boring until I realized how it applies to everything around us
  • Technology: Learning to code and understanding how digital tools can enhance our learning
  • English: Reading great books and learning to express ideas clearly through writing
  • History: Understanding how past events shape our present world

My Interests Outside School

When I'm not studying, you can find me:

  • Reading science fiction novels (currently working through Isaac Asimov's Foundation series)
  • Playing soccer with friends
  • Learning web development (I actually built this website myself!)
  • Playing video games (especially strategy and puzzle games)
  • Volunteering at the local library during weekends

My Goals

Through this blog, I hope to:

  • Document my learning journey and reflect on my growth
  • Share study tips and strategies that work for middle school students
  • Show how modern technology is changing education
  • Inspire other students to embrace learning and curiosity
  • Connect with students, teachers, and parents who share similar interests

What Makes My School Experience "Modern"?

Our school embraces technology in ways that make learning more engaging and relevant:

  • We use tablets for note-taking and research
  • Virtual reality experiences for history and geography lessons
  • Online collaboration tools for group projects
  • Digital portfolios to track our progress
  • Coding classes starting from Year 7
  • 3D printing in our design technology classes

Fun Facts About Me

  • I can solve a Rubik's cube in under 2 minutes
  • My favorite subject used to be art, but now it's chemistry
  • I've read all the Harry Potter books three times
  • I built my first computer last summer with my dad's help
  • I'm learning Spanish as my second language
  • My dream job is to become a software engineer or maybe a science teacher

Why I Blog

Writing about my experiences helps me process what I'm learning and reflect on my growth. Plus, if my experiences can help even one other student feel less alone in their educational journey, then it's all worth it.

I believe that every student's perspective is valuable, and sharing our stories helps create a community of learners who support each other.

Connect With Me

I'd love to hear from other students, teachers, or anyone interested in modern education! Feel free to reach out if you have questions, want to share your own experiences, or just want to chat about school life.

Thanks for reading, and I hope you enjoy following my journey through Year 8 and beyond!
